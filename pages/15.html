2011-15 Batch Placement news:



Dream Offers

Opening up the Dream Offer option for students since the 2012-2013 batch has immensely helped them to bag the dream package they aspired to. All the placed students may get one more offer from a Core company or a Software company that offered a better package Software companies that offer more than 4 lakhs per annum are offered the ‘Dream status’.
Companies like Flipkart, Intel, Akamai, Cisco, Yahoo, Oracle, Google, KLA Tencor, Dolcera have recruited the students with packages of more than Rs. 7,00,000 per annum.
